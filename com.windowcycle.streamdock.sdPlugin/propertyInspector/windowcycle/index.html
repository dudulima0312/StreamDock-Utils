How many ticks to navigate (1 = most sensitive)

Navigation Options

Inverts the knob rotation direction
Press the knob to confirm window selection

How to Use

With Knob:
• Rotate to open Task View and navigate
• Press to select current window
• Stop rotating to auto-select after delay

With Button:
• Press to open Task View
• Press again to navigate
• Wait for auto-select or press knob

Note: This plugin simulates keyboard shortcuts (Alt+Tab). Make sure StreamDock has proper permissions to send keyboard inputs.
